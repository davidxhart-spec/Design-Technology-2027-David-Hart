IB Design Technology 2027 — Student Textbook

A complete student companion for the IB Diploma Programme Design Technology course (first assessment 2027).

How to use this book

Each chapter covers one syllabus topic. Chapters are structured identically:

  1. Key concepts — the core content you need to understand
  2. Case studies — real products that illustrate the concepts
  3. Glossary — key terms and definitions
  4. Check your understanding — practice questions with model answers

Work through chapters in order, or dip into specific topics as they arise in class. Questions at the end of each chapter are written in the style of IB Paper 1 and Paper 2 exam items.
